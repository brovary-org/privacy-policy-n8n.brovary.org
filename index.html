Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 19, 2025

Important Notice: This is a self-hosted n8n instance. All data is processed and stored locally on our infrastructure. We do not share your data with n8n AG or any third parties.

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how our self-hosted n8n workflow automation instance handles your data. n8n is an open-source workflow automation tool that we host on our own infrastructure to maintain full control over your data.

This application integrates with Google services via OAuth and complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

2. Google API Services and OAuth Integration

Our n8n instance may access your Google account data when you authorize workflows to connect with Google services. This section describes how we handle information received from Google APIs.

2.1 Google Data We May Access

Depending on the workflows you create and authorize, we may access:

  • Gmail: Send and read emails, manage drafts, and access email metadata
  • Google Drive: Create, read, update, and manage files and folders
  • Google Calendar: Create, read, and manage calendar events
  • Google Sheets: Read and write spreadsheet data
  • Google Docs: Create and edit documents
  • Google Contacts: Access and manage contact information
  • Profile Information: Basic profile information (name, email address)

2.2 How We Use Google Data

Google user data accessed through our n8n instance is used solely to:

  • Execute the specific workflow automations that you create and authorize
  • Perform the operations you explicitly configure in your workflows
  • Display information within the n8n interface for workflow configuration and monitoring

We do NOT:

  • Use Google data for any purpose other than executing your authorized workflows
  • Transfer Google data to third parties (except as necessary to execute your workflows and as disclosed in this policy)
  • Use Google data for serving advertisements
  • Allow humans to read your Google data, except with your explicit consent for support purposes or as required by law

2.3 Google API Services User Data Policy Compliance

This application's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. This means:

  • We only request access to the specific Google data needed for your workflows
  • We use the data only for the purposes you authorize
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your Google data
  • We do not transfer your Google data to others except as necessary to provide the functionality you request

2.4 Revoking Google Access

You can revoke this application's access to your Google account at any time by:

  • Visiting your Google Account Permissions page
  • Locating this application in the list of connected apps
  • Clicking "Remove Access"

Alternatively, you can disconnect Google credentials within the n8n instance under the Credentials section.

3. Data Controller

This n8n instance is operated and controlled by our organization. We are the data controller responsible for your personal information processed through this service.

4. Data We Collect

When you use this n8n instance, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and authentication credentials
  • Workflow Data: Workflows you create, including nodes, configurations, and connections
  • Execution Data: Workflow execution logs and results
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the platform
  • OAuth Credentials: Access tokens for connected services (stored securely and encrypted)

5. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain the n8n workflow automation service
  • Authenticate and authorize users
  • Execute and monitor workflows
  • Troubleshoot issues and improve service performance
  • Ensure security and prevent unauthorized access
  • Connect to authorized third-party services via OAuth

6. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored on our self-hosted infrastructure. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Backup and disaster recovery procedures
  • Encrypted storage of OAuth credentials and sensitive data

7. Data Sharing

Since this is a self-hosted instance:

  • Your data is NOT sent to n8n AG or their cloud services
  • We do NOT share your data with third parties except when required by law or when you explicitly authorize workflows to interact with external services
  • Data remains within our infrastructure at all times, except when transmitted to services you've authorized in your workflows
  • External API calls made by your workflows are under your control and follow your configured authorization

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Workflow execution logs may be retained for a limited period for troubleshooting purposes. Google API data is not permanently stored except as required to execute your workflows.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your workflows and data
  • Object to data processing
  • Revoke OAuth access to connected services at any time

10. Cookies and Tracking

This n8n instance uses essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use third-party tracking or analytics cookies.

11. Third-Party Integrations

When you create workflows that connect to external services (APIs, databases, Google services, etc.), those connections are established based on your explicit authorization. Each third-party service you integrate with may have its own privacy policy and terms of service that you should review.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the platform or via email.

13. Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact your system administrator or the designated data protection contact for this instance.
